Welcome to my currently playing section!



Here is a quick summation of what I am playing and my current thoughts on it! I hope you enjoy!

Elden Ring

I have been running back through Elden Ring with a new character as they just announced the new DLC which got me very excited. Easily one of my top favorite games of all time.

Granblue Fantasy: Relink

My most recent obsession. I picked this up when it released having never played a Granblue game before and I have been in love with it since. Its cleverly disguised as a pseudo-mmo.

YuGiOh! Duel Masters

Oh boy, I have been playing this with my friends back home and it is a nostalgic and often frustrating trip down memory lane. I Highly Highly recommend as it is free! The UI leaves a lot to be desired but once you are in a duel its perfect.
